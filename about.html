About Me

Hey there! I'm Jeniah Clarke, a web designer, artist, and all-around creative soul based in the vibrant city of San Antonio, Texas. Whether I'm buried in a good book, jotting down my thoughts, or letting my imagination run wild on canvas, I find joy in every moment of it.

With a Bachelor of Science in Communications Film & Theater under my belt, I'm currently diving into the world of Digital Media with an Associate's degree. Why limit myself, right? I'm a firm believer in exploring every avenue of creativity, which is why you'll find me dabbling in everything from filmmaking to web design.

Storytelling is my passion. There's something magical about weaving narratives that captivate hearts and minds, whether it's through the lens of a camera or the strokes of a paintbrush. My goal is simple: to use my skills and creativity to elevate others' projects and bring their visions to life.

Growing up as a military brat instilled in me a love for adventure and a curiosity about the world around me. That's what led me to take a leap and study abroad in South Korea. Immersing myself within a different culture, soaking up its history and art, and forging friendships along the way was an experience I'll cherish forever.

As I continue to pursue my dreams within the film industry and beyond, I'm excited to see where this creative journey takes me next. Whether it's behind the camera, behind the keyboard, or behind the scenes, you can bet I'll be there, ready to tell stories and make magic happen.
